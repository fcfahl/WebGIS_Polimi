Land Cover European Urban Atlas 2006
Institutions European Commission Directorate-General for Regional Policy and the Directorate-General for Enterprise and Industry with the support of the European Space Agency and the European Environment Agency
Temporal coverage 2005-2007
Spatial Coverage Europe
Sensor Earth Observation (EO) data with 2.5 m spatial resolution multispectral or pan-sharpened (multispectral merged with panchromatic) data
Input Data Earth Observation (EO) data
Topographic Maps at a scale of 1: 50 000 or larger
COTS navigation data for the road network
Ancillary data COTS navigation data: points of interest, land use, land cover, water areas
Google Earth (only for interpretation, not for delineation)
Local city maps
Local zoning data
Field check (on-site visit)
Very high resolution imagery (better than 1 m ground resolution, e.g. aerial photographs)
Scale of the data set 1:10.000
Native Projection ETRS-LAEA (EPSG:3035)
Classification Scheme Thematic classes based on CORINE LC nomenclature and GUS Legend.
Thematic accuracy (in %) Minimum overall accuracy for level 1 class 1 “Artificial surfaces”: 85%
Minimum overall accuracy (all classes): 80%
Positional Accuracy + / - 5 m
